Equipment Recommendations

What's In My Kitchen

After 12 years in Michelin-recognized professional kitchens, I have strong opinions about equipment. These are the exact pieces I use, recommend without hesitation, and believe will make the biggest difference in your home cooking — organized by what matters most.

Affiliate note: links below are Amazon affiliate links. I earn a small commission if you purchase — at no extra cost to you. I only list things I would genuinely buy myself.

The 80/20 of Kitchen Equipment

You don't need a fully equipped professional kitchen. You need four things that do most of the work: a 10–12" stainless steel or cast iron skillet, a quality chef's knife, a heavy Dutch oven, and an instant-read thermometer. Everything else is an upgrade. If you only buy four things from this page, buy those four.
